What Is Fresh Alum

Fresh Alum aims to provide users with a way to create valuable and long lasting relationships between current Howard students and alumni.

Our team consists of four Howard students. We noticed a disconnect between our current student body and alumni and wanted to use our senior project to change that. We figured providing students and alumni with a direct way of communicating would do just that. So, Fresh Alum was born; An environment for students and alumni to connect, communicate, and create meaningful relationships.
